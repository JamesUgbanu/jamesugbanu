# Case Studies

These case studies are written as public portfolio documents rather than internal architecture notes. They are intentionally sanitized for public viewing and focus on engineering decisions, tradeoffs, implementation strategy, and business impact.

## Included Projects

- [TaxBloc](taxbloc/README.md)
  Financial export platform work focused on backend systems, cloud deployment maturity, and controlled scalability.
- [myTreep](mytreep/README.md)
  Founder-led product engineering around AI-assisted travel planning, operational flexibility, and monetization design.
- [TribeIRL](tribeirl/README.md)
  Software engineering within a community and events platform with broad domain complexity and production-facing workflows.

## What These Writeups Emphasize

- clear problem framing
- engineering constraints and tradeoffs
- production readiness and reliability thinking
- scalability and infrastructure judgment
- business value, not just technology lists

Each writeup is intended to be readable by both technical and non-technical reviewers while still reflecting real engineering depth.
